About Me

Hello! I’m Salome Kendi, a Special Needs Teacher with a Master’s in Advanced Teaching. I’m passionate about inclusive learning and supporting learners with diverse needs including visual impairments, ADHD, and language learning challenges.

My Teaching Philosophy

I believe every learner can thrive when teaching is adapted to their strengths and needs. I use creative strategies and AI-powered tools to make learning accessible and engaging.
